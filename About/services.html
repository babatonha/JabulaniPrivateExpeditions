About Our Services

We here at Jabulani Private Tours are an inbound agency specializing in facilitating private, socially and economically responsible tourism in Zimbabwe, Botswana and Namibia. We take delight in ensuring that our clients get to experience the best of what Africa has to offer. Our goal is to make sure your expedition is as seamless as possible,and we fully leverage the collective experience, passion and commitment of our team to see to all your travel needs.
